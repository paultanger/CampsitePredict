About me

I am a passionate data scientist with a comprehensive background and record in modeling, statistics, data management and programming. I am really interested in applying modern techniques to data and challenges in agriculture and beyond.

During my graduate research in agriculture, I discovered my love for data and uncovering insight and value from messy data and helping others manage and uncover meaning from their data. My favorite part was the analysis and discovery not necessarily in the lab generating the data. After completing my research, I contributed to the "big picture" managing government supported projects in DC but my true passion is buried in the data - making sense of it.

I look forward to continuing to explore and implement modern techniques for unique applications. Please check my Github and LinkedIn and reach out if you have common interests!
